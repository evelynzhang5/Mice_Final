Estrus Rhythm Explorer

Visualizing mouse activity and temperature across ovulation cycles.

Project Write-Up

This visualization lets you “race” mouse activity and temperature across a two-week (14-day) cycle. Play/Pause controls animate each minute for all subjects simultaneously. You can toggle between activity and temperature views, and hovering on a dot shows the exact value.

We began by loading per-minute CSVs for activity and temperature and mapping them into a single data structure keyed by mouse ID. Scales and axes were created with D3’s linear and category helpers. A render loop updates positions each “tick” and handles user controls. Finally, we added tooltips and color-coding for clarity.

Potential next steps include letting users select individual mice to highlight, comparing estrus vs. non-estrus days side by side, or overlaying light/dark cycles as background bands.
